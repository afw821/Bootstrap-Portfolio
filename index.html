About Me

My name is Alex Watkins. I'm married and live with my wife, Crystal Watkins, in Atlanta, Ga. I grew up close by in Roswell, Ga. I graduated from Roswell High School in 2005 and attended Auburn University where I majored in Criminology and Criminal Justice. I received my Bachelor's degree from Auburn University and began my career in Law Enforcement. I was a Police Officer in Montgomery, Al for two years and a Deputy Sheriff at Cherokee County, Ga for 3 1/2 years. I decided to make a career changee and left my job as a Deputy and am currently attending Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp where I expect to graduate in December 2018. I am currently seeking full-time employment in the technology field. In my spare time I enjoy hanging out with my wife, my friends and my family. I enjoy watching Braves Baseball and love college football. I enjoy traveling and anything involving the outdoors. Kayaking, hiking, camping, fishing and mountain biking are among some of my favorite outdoor activities. I really enjoy music and love going to concerts with friends.
